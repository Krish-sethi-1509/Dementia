Why Early Detection Matters

Recognizing the early signs of dementia is crucial. While most types are irreversible, an early diagnosis allows for timely intervention, which can help manage symptoms and slow progression. It empowers individuals and families to plan for the future, access support systems, and improve the overall quality of life.
